Some of my friends looked like they had life figured out on the outside, but behind the scenes they were dealing with pressure, identity confusion, and decisions that didn&rsquo;t match the life they said they wanted.

Over time, I realized something simple. <span class="text-ink font-medium">It wasn&rsquo;t a lack of potential. It was a lack of clarity.</span>

Young & Aligned started as personal reflection notes and journaling pages while I was trying to make sense of those patterns. As I began working with young people, I kept seeing the same thing: when people understand themselves clearly, they make better decisions. And when they make better decisions, their direction becomes clearer.
