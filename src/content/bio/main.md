<span class="text-ink font-medium">Bimbo Ogunsola is a Workforce Analyst, Speaker, Human Development Strategist, and Founder of Young & Aligned.</span>

She helps young people gain clarity about who they are, think more clearly, and make intentional decisions aligned with the life they want to build.

She is the creator of the Young & Aligned Framework (Awaken, Transform, Master), a structured system that helps people understand themselves, shift how they think, and take intentional action in their daily lives.

Her work is shaped by early experiences of seeing people appear fine on the outside while quietly dealing with pressure, confusion, and uncertainty about their direction. A defining moment was witnessing how quickly life can shift when people lack clarity in their relationships and decisions, which was what deepened her commitment to helping young people slow down and think clearly before making life-shaping choices.

Since then, she has supported individuals through speaking and guided sessions that help them gain clarity, reframe how they think, and take ownership of their direction.
